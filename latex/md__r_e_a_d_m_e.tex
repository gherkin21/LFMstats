A desktop application built with C++ and the Qt framework to fetch, store, analyze, and visualize your Last.\+fm scrobble history.\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Description}{Description}}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md1}
This application connects to the Last.\+fm API to download your music listening history (scrobbles). It stores the data efficiently in a local database (weekly JSON files) and provides various tools to analyze and visualize your listening habits, including\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Overall statistics (first/last scrobble, streaks, average plays/day)
\item Top artist and track rankings
\item Charts showing listening patterns over time (hourly, weekly)
\item Ability to search for the last time you played a specific track.
\end{DoxyItemize}

It supports fetching your complete history, performing incremental updates, and resuming interrupted downloads.\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Features}{Features}}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md2}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Full History Fetch\+:}} Download your entire Last.\+fm scrobble history.
\item {\bfseries{Incremental Updates\+:}} Fetch only new scrobbles since the last sync.
\item {\bfseries{Download Resumption\+:}} Resumes fetching from the last successfully saved page if the initial download is interrupted.
\item {\bfseries{Local Database\+:}} Stores scrobbles locally in JSON files organized by week per user.
\item {\bfseries{Dashboard Stats\+:}} View total scrobbles, date range, average scrobbles per day, and listening streaks.
\item {\bfseries{Last Played Finder\+:}} Search for the last time you listened to a specific artist/track combination.
\item {\bfseries{Top Lists\+:}} See your most played artists and tracks.
\item {\bfseries{Charts\+:}}
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Top 10 Artists (Bar Chart)
\item Top 10 Tracks (Bar Chart)
\item Scrobbles per Hour of Day (Bar Chart)
\item Scrobbles per Day of Week (Bar Chart)
\end{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Background Operations\+:}} Fetching and saving happen in background threads to keep the UI responsive.
\item {\bfseries{User Configuration\+:}} Easily set and change your Last.\+fm username and API key.
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md3}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Prerequisites}{Prerequisites}}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md3}
